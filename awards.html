My Awards

Winning Medal and Award points and earning discounts.

Each day that you do 10,000 steps and use your Activ8rlives step counter to record and upload your activity to your account, you earn a point towards your medals and a point you can donate to one of your groups.

  • Earn a Bronze Medal for every 7 points earned.
  • Earn a Silver Medal for every 5 bronze medals (or 35 points) and get 5-20% off all Activ8rlives products (5% for each of your first four Silver Medals).
  • Earn a Gold Medal for every 5 silver medals (175 points ) and get 30% off all Activ8rlives products when you reach your first Gold Medal.

Other benefits include membership of our Silver and Gold Medal groups, giving you priority support and early access to new products and services.

Badges

Badges will be awarded for certain milestones. These could be for distance walked, regulary measuring/uploading your data and many more. Share your badges on Facebook and Twitter to show your friends how well you are doing.

Your badges awarded will be in colour below and the ones you are working towards are in grey. Click on the badges to see what you need to earn them or share them if already earned. Special badges will be availible to earn and will be awarded for great achievers, group goals etc. Keep an eye out for new badges you can earn.
